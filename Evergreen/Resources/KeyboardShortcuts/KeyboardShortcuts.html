Evergreen has a number of keyboard shortcuts that aren’t listed in the menubar.

The key to reading your news while holding your coffe is the space bar. The space bar scrolls the current article — and, when it’s scrolled all the way, it goes to the next unread article.

This way you can go through all your news via the space bar.
